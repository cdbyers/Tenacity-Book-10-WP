A5.1 Introduction: What do you know about advertisements?
People use advertisements to offer something for sale, to find out what is available, to
warn or inform others. Advertisements can be found in the newspapers, on the radio and
television, on the Internet, in medical leaflets or on public display boards (bill boards).
