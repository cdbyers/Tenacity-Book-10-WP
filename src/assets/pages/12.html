A1.9 Writing: Your favourite sport
Work in groups to write some paragraphs to describe your favourite sport, following the
seven stages of writing: think, brainstorm, plan, draft, check, re-write, proofread. Include
your reasons why young people should do exercise and try to persuade them to take up your
chosen sport.
Suggested paragraph topics might be:
Introduction
Favourite sport
Why exercise is important, etc.
