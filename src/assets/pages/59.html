2
Use who, whom, whose, that or which to join these sentences.
1 The girl is fetching water from the well. She is my friend.
2 This is my aunt. I was telling you about her.
3 Where is the book? I put it on the table.
4 That is the man. His daughter is getting married next week.
5 Do you know the man? They are talking about him.
6 Have you seen the doctor? She saved my mother’s life.
7 That is the junction. The accident occurred there.
8 I like bananas. It is why my mother gave me some for lunch.
