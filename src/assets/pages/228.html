We noticed = an example of the past simple.
We were all looking for something = an example of the past continuous.
A huge male lion had walked up to the bus = an example of the past perfect.
We use these different verb forms to add interest to a story. If we used only one verb form all the
time, for example when telling a story, it would be very dull.
The past continuous gives us information about what was going on in the background against which
the events of the story happened.
