B10.4 Speaking: Alternative sources of power
1
Look at this sentence:
Instead of getting energy from electricity, a house could get power from solar panels.
Notice the pattern instead of + -ing in the sentence. Listen to your teacher who will give you some other
examples of sentences using this pattern. Practise saying these sentences in pairs.
2
Work in groups to discuss alternative means of powering things, using this pattern.
3
In your group, discuss what kind of energy Ethiopia should invest in: hydro-electric power,
nuclear energy, fossil fuels, etc using the information from the reading texts. Use the modal
verbs could, may, might (see Unit 4 A4.3) to make your suggestions.
Examples: Ethiopia could invest in wind power technology.
Solar power might be cheaper than oil.
Nuclear power may not be the answer to Ethiopia’s energy problems.
