A3.5 Listening: Ben and the Devil
1
Listen to the first part of the story of Ben and the Devil.
1 Choose a sentence to complete the first section of the story.
a His wife was so cross that she decided to run away.
b Suddenly he saw a dark stranger standing in front of him.
It was the Devil himself.
c ‘You should not call on the Devil,’ his wife said. ‘You
should work harder.’
2 Choose a sentence to complete the second part of the story.
a On the anniversary of the day that Ben had received the
gold, the Devil reappeared.
b Ben and his wife grew fat and lazy and Ben never had to
work again.
c Ben had spent all his money. He hoped the Devil would
give him some more.
3 Choose a sentence to end the story.
a They had many children and lived in a big house with
many servants.
b Ben’s wife never complained again so Ben was a happy
man.
c Years later when the shop was pulled down, they built a
hotel called ‘The Disappearing Devil’.
