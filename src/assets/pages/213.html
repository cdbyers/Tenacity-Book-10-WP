A12.1 Introduction: Ups and downs
1

things that can make you sad.
2

A12.2 Listening: Weddings
Around Africa, there are many different marriage traditions. Marriage is extremely important,
joining not just two people, but two families, and even two communities. There are traditions
concerning how the arrangements for the wedding are made, whether or not a dowry or bride price
is paid, how the ceremony is conducted and what happens afterwards.
