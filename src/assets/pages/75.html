B4.4 Language focus: Direct and indirect objects
A direct object answers the question What? while an indirect object answers the question To whom?
or For whom? Indirect objects are usually found with verbs of giving or communicating like lend,
give, bring, tell, show, take, or offer. An indirect object is always a noun or pronoun.
Example:
Give me the book.
