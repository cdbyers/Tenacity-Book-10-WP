But then:
We met on the battlefield
Where corpses were the signs of victory
And vultures yearned for their harvest,
And my clean sword longed for a stain
And the gentle in me craved to be cruel.
And so I tore into his bowels
And felt strengthened by the scent of his blood.
And now by the nature of steel
I fearlessly committed the unforgiveable sin.
Silas Obadiah
