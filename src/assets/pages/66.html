A4.5 Language focus: Right and wrong
1
Work in your group to write down three examples of ‘wrong’ behaviour and three examples
of ‘right’ behaviour.
Examples:
Stealing is wrong.
Helping others is right.
Gerunds are nouns formed from verbs. They are formed by adding -ing to verbs:
walking, talking, thinking, listening
Like nouns, gerunds can be the subject, object or complement of a sentence:
●
Caring for others is right.
●
I don’t like cheating.
●
My favourite occupation is reading.
