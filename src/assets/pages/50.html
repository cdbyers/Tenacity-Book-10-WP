B3.3 Speaking: A radio report
Work in small groups.
1
Learn these social expressions for giving a report:
First of all …
To begin with …
Then …
After that …
Finally …
To cut a long story short …
So in the end …
All in all …
2
Choose a special event that has happened at your school or in your town that everyone in
the group can remember. It could be your last school speech day or sports day, a national
celebration, or a religious festival.
3
Talk about exactly what happened that day:
who was there
●
the events of the day
●
who made speeches and (if you can remember) what they spoke about
●
anything special that happened
●
how long it lasted
●
when it finished.
