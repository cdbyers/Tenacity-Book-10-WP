B12.7 Language focus: Present perfect and past simple tenses
In Unit 11, A11.10, we looked at using the present perfect to denote a period of time and the past
simple tense to denote a particular time.
Examples:
He’s never been to Gonder. (present perfect)
He went to Axum in 1999. (past simple)
