B9.8 Increase your word power: Word building
You know many more words than you think!
We can change the form of many words in English by adding an ending. For example:
We can make nouns from some verbs by adding -ion, -ation, -ent, -er.
We can make nouns from some adjectives by adding -ness, -ity.
