A9.5 Speaking: A guessing game
1
Listen to your teacher using a number of expressions to guess what an object might be.
2
Work in pairs. Look at each picture and take it in turns to decide what it shows, using the
following expressions:
I’d say …
Could it be …?
Perhaps it’s …
I think it’s …
It looks like …
It’s difficult to say, but I’d guess …
